比字句用法 泰国普吉孔院原创微课 夏蓓（下）

2017-4-19阅读（1,064）

15014


A比B + adj + （数量number）

 

例句

Examples

 

哥哥比我大三岁。 (My brother is three years older than me.)

爸爸比我大30岁。(My father is 30 years older than me.)

 

A比B +一点儿(a little)/得多（很多）(much)

 

例句

Examples

 

哥哥比我大一点。(My brother is a little older than me.)

爸爸比我大的多。(My father is much older than me.)
